FAQ's

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization and refers to the process of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). A higher ranking in SERPs can lead to increased traffic and exposure for a website, which can, in turn, lead to increased revenue and ROI for a business.

There are many common myths about SEO, such as that it is easy to do, unimportant, or only about keywords. However, SEO is not easy, it is important, and while keywords are one part of it, there is much more to consider when optimizing a website.

SEO is not an instant fix; it can take a few months or even years to see the results of your efforts. However, if you are patient and consistent with your optimization efforts, you will eventually see a rise in your website's SERP ranking. But with us, you can rest assured to see results not too late!

Yes, in case you are looking to increase your sales and bring in organic traffic, SEO is the best bet to invest in. The only catch is to work with a professional SEO agency because only the professional knows how to make the optimization efforts work.

On-page SEO refers to the optimization of a website's individual pages to rank higher in SERPs. This includes factors such as the page title, Meta tags, and content.

Off-page SEO refers to optimizing a website's external factors to rank higher in SERPs. This includes link building, social media signals, and reviews.
